-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
----     Graphics
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The game features the work of DragonDePlatino and his DawnLike tileset, available at:
	http://opengameart.org/content/dawnlike-16x16-universal-rogue-like-tileset-v18
This work is modified, but mostly through some minor color changes.  The work is nearly all still
	DragonDePlatino's
	
Credit goes to DawnBringer, whose DB 16 pallete served as the original palette for DawnLike, and whose
DB 32 palette is the basis for the current game.  The DB 32 palette is available at:
	http://www.pixeljoint.com/forum/forum_posts.asp?TID=16247
The platino sprite is not yet hidden in the game, but it will be before 1.0 :).

All graphical assets in the game are DawnLike, except:
 * Title title window border (by JAP at opengameart.org/content/borderdecoration, licensed under
	CC-BY-SA 3.0) (modified by me)
 * Main Menu GUI elemtns (by Lamoot at opengameart.org/content/rpg-gui-construction-kit-v10, licensed
	under CC-BY 3.0) (modified by me)
 * The character sprites (done by me).
 * Other assets from the public domain.
